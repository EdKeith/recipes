Strawberries and Mint Creme
===========================

4 cups heavy cream
1/2 pkg fresh mint
1 Qt. Strawberries
1/2 cup Zinfandel
6 pepper corns
i tsp. fennel seeds
1 cardamom pod
3 Tbsp. sugar

Bring cream to a biol and immediately submerge mint, remove from heat and cover
and let steep for 30 minutes. Cool and discard mint.

Bring 1 pt. strawberries and all remaining ingredients to a boil and simmer 30
minutes. Cool and discard spices.

Slice 1 pt. strawberries.

Divide cooked and sliced strawberries into 6 servings and top each serving with
mint cream and garnish with mint leaves.
