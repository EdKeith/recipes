Pumpkin Pie Pudding

Addapted fro  Puddin'
by Clio Goodman


3 1/2 cups milk
3 egg yolks
3 Tbsp corn Starch
15 oz canned pumkin
1 cup dark brown sugar
1 tsp salt
1/8 tsp ground nutmeg
1/8 tsp ground ginger
1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
1 tsp vanilla extract

mix all but vinilla in saucepan
heat over medium stirring constantly untill starts to thicke.
remove from heat and stir in vanilla
