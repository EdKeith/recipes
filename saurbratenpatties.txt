Sauerbraten Patties
-------------------

Mix 

3 slices stale bread - crumbles
1 lb. ground beef
1 onion, minced
1 egg
1 1/2 tsp. salt

and form into patties. brown in oil

Add
 1/3 cup vinegar
 1 1/2 cups water
 8 ginger snaps
 10 cloves
 5 bay leaves

cover and simmer one hour.
