Tart Cranberry Casserole
========================

3 cups Dices Granny Smith Apples
2 cups raw cranberries
1 cup sugar

1 stick margarine
1 cup oats
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/4 cup flour
1 cup chopped pecans

Mix Apples, Cranberries, and sugar and spread in bottom of casserole dish

Mix oats, brown sugar, flour, & pecans.

Melt margarine and mix into oat mixture.

Sprinkle out mixture over apples/cranberry mixture

Cover and bake 1 1/4 hours at 350.

Remove cover and brown before serving.
