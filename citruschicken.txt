Citrus-Marinated Chicken


from Simply Mexican by Lourdes Castro

Too much cumin


2 1/2 lbs. chicken
1 yellow onion, halved
2 cloves garlic
1 tsp. salt
2 Tbsp. oregano
1 cup O.J.
1/2 cup lime juice (~5 limes) + limes for garnish
1 Large red onion, thinly sliced
2 jalapenos, stemmed & sliced into rings
1 tbsp cumin
1/4 cup olive oil

Put chicken, yellow onion, garlic, salt & 1 Tbsp. Oragano in sauce pan &
barly cover w/ water.
Bring to boil, reduce heat & simmer uncovered 30 minutes.
Allow to cool in broth.
Strain & reserve broth.

Combine O.J, lime juice, red onion, jalapenos, cumin & remaining oragano, in
sauce pan, stir well.
Bring to boil quickly.
Remove from heat & add olive oil.
Put marinade & chicken in bowl & marinade 30 minutes to 1 day, covered with
plastic, in refrigerator.

Serve cold or at room temperature.

serves 6
