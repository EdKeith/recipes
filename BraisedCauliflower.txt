Braised Cauliflour

from
The Mediterranean Diet Cookbook for Beginners
by Elena Paravantes

1/2 cup olive oil
1 head cauliflower, cut into mediun-sized florets
1 medium white potato, cut into 1" cubes
1/4 tsp ground black peppeer
3 allspice berries
1 cinnamon stick
3 cloves
2 TBSP tomato paste
1 tsp salt
3/4 cup hot water

heat olive oil over med heat till it shimmers.

Add cauliflower, potatoes, and spices. Saute 4 minutes, until cauliflower starts to brown.

Add tomatoe paate and salt. countinue to heat untill brick red.

Add hot watere, simmer on low for 30 minutes until tender.

remove from heat and let stand at least 10 minutes.


Notes:

think about parcooking potatoes in microwave a few minutes.

think about adding some red wine after tomatoe poste is cooked and reducing it before adding the water.

think about adding green beens.
