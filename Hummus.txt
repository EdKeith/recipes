Hummus     2/ 2009

2 lb. to 1 lb. 13 oz. chick peas 1 large can or 2 small cans

1 Tbsp garlic
1 1/2 Tb balsamic vinegar, less acidic version
2 Tbsp lemon juice, may substitute 1 Tb acidic balsamic vinegar
1 1/2 Tbsp sesame oil
3 Tbsp olive oil
2 Tbsp Tahini
2 to 5 Tbsp water

1 tsp salt
1/8 tsp sumac,  may need to double this if it is old
1 1/2 tsp mint
1/2 tsp cinnamon
1/32 tsp cayenne
< 1/4 tsp black pepper

Mix everything in a food processor. The flavors blend and
develop over the first few hours. Adjust the lemon juice and
balsamic vinegar  to taste.
