Green Beans w/ Cranberries and Walnuts
======================================

1 lb. frozen green beans
1/2 tsp. oil
1/4 cup chopped cranberries
2 Tbsp. Walnuts
1 Tbsp. Honey

Microwave beans on high until tender, about 6 minutes.

cook cranberries and walnuts in oil until cranberries are tender.

toss bean, cranberry mixture and honey and serve hot.
