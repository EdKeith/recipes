Red Pepper Jelly
================

2 cups sweet red pepper seeded and chopped fine
5 1/2 cups sugar
3/4 cup vinegar
1/4 cup water
1/3 cup lemon juice
1 bottle pectin

boil peppers, sugar, vinegar, and water stirring constantly.
let cool 15 minutes and reheat to boiling again and biol 2 minutes.
remove from heat and add pectin, skim, and stir occasionally for 5 minutes
poor int jars and cover with wax.
let age min. 2 weeks.
