Roquefort Soufflé
-----------------

2 TBSP butter
1/4 cup flour
3/4 cup milk
1/4 cup dry white wine
4 oz. crumbled Roquefort cheese
1/8 tsp pepper
Dash nutmeg
3 egg yolks, lightly beaten
6 egg whites

Butter and collar a 1.5 Qt soufflé dish.

Melt butter in sauce pan and stir in flour.
add milk & wine sirring over med. heat until think.
Add Cheese, pepper and nutmeg.
Remove from heat and stir in yolks.

Beat whites to soft peaks and fold into cheese mixture.

Pour into prepared dish and bake at 350 for 30 min.
