Pepperines

1 1/2 cups flour
1/2 tsp salt
1 tsp pepper
1 tsp sugar
2 Tbsp. oil
1/4 - 1/2 cups water
2Tbsp lemon Juice

heat oven to 350.

mix flour, salt, pepper & sugar. Add oil & Blend until resembles coarse meal.

combine 1/4 cup water and the lemmon juice & blend into flour mixture. add
enough water to form a ball.

roll to 1/16" thick, prick w/ fork and bake 15-20 minutes
