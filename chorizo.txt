Mexican Chorizo

from Simply Mexican by Lourdes Castro

Untested

2 cloves garlic
1 Tbsp salt
1/2 cup red wine vinagar
1/2 tsp black pepper
1 Tbsp oregano
1 tsp sugar
3 Tbsp ancho chile powder
1 canned chipotle
1 Tbsp adobo sauce from canned chipotle
2 Tbsp water
1 lb. ground beef
1lb. ground pork

Combine garlic, salt, vineger, black peper, oregano, sugar, chile powder,
chipotle, adobo sauce & water in blender & puree.

Put meat in a bowl
add spice mixture
work the spices until well blended.
Shape into log & roll securly in parchiment paper twisting ends closed,
Alternatly seal in a plastic bag
Refrigerte overnight.


makes 5 cups

use in 2 days or
freeze up to 3 month in 1/2 cup portions
