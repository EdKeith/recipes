Avgolemono

From Pucker: schoolbook for citrus lovers, by Gwendolyn Richards


8 cups chicken stock
1 cup oral
4 eggs, separated
Zest of one lemon
Juice of three lemons
1 1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp black pepper

Bring stock to boil, add oral and boil 6-7 minutes

Meanwhile whip egg whites to soft peaks
Beat yolks, zest & juice to whites.
When pasta is cooked, slowly pore 2 cups of hot broth into whiles beating at low speed to temper the eggs.

Whisk egg mixture into remaining stock.

Add salt and pepper and serve immediately.
