Spiced Angle food Cake w/ Cranberry Topping
------------------------------------------

Add 
    1 tsp. Cinnamon
    1/4 tsp. ginger
    1/4 tsp. nutmeg
to dry angle food cake mix and prepare as directed.

mix
  2 cups cranberries
  1 1/2 cup sugar
  1/2 cup water
  1/2 tsp cinnamon
  1/4 tsp nutmeg
  1 1/2 tsp grated orange zest
bring to boil and cook 3 - 5 minute, until cranberries pop
remove from heat and add
   2 tsp. corn starch mixed with
   2 Tbsp Orange juice
Cook 5 minutes more
cool

Serve cake toped with sauce.
