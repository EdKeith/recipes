Spaghetti al Limond

From Pucker: a cookbook for citrus lovers, by Gwendolyn Richards

Untested

1lb spaghetti or other long pasta
2 lemmons, seated & juiced
1egg yolk
1/3 cups whipping cream
3 Tbsp olive oil
1 cup grated Parmesan
Ground black pepper
Salt
2 - 3 Tbsp parsley chopped


Cooking pasta

whisk together zest, yolk, cream, oil, 3/4 cup Parmesan, and a few grinds of pepper.
Mix in 1/4 cup juice.

When the pasta is cooked reserve 1 cup cooking water. Drain pasta. Return to pot. Add back 1/4 cup cooking water and toss to coat.

Toss with lemon mixture.

Add more cooking water if it seems dry.

Serve topped with paddler and Parmesan.
