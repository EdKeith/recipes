Citrus pickled onions

From Pucker: a cookbook for citrus lovers, by Gwendolyn Richards

1/2 cup white vinegar
Juice of 2 limes
Juice of 2 lemons
1/3 cup sugar
5 cloves
5 peppercorns
1/4 tsp mustard seeds
1/4 tsp coriander seeds
1/4 tsp salt
1 bay leaf
1 red onion, cut in half, thinly sliced

In small saucepan over medium low heat, combine vinegar, lemon, lime, sugar, spices, salt and bay leaf.
Stir occasionally till sugar is dissolved
Add onions stir.
Cook until soft and bright pink, about 1-2 minutes
Let cool then refrigerate
