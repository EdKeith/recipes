Peanutbutter Pudding

Addapted from Puddin' by Clio Goodman

4 1/4 cups milk
1 1/2 cups peanutbutter
3/4 cup sugar
1/4 cup corn starch
1/2 tsp salt

mix all
heat slowly while stirring constantly unto thick.



Ideas:
original recipie called for part cream

try replacing peanutbutter with chocolate and cocoa powder and pepermit extract.
