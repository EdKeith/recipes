Easy Butter Mints
=================

1/4 cup butter
2 Tbsp. water
1/4 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. pepermint extract
3 1/2 - 3 1/4 cofectioners sugar

melt butter in water, remove from heat.

add salt and pepermint

gradualt add sugar to desiredconsistancy.

kneed untill smooth roll into small logs. (rolls will keep in refrigerator)

slice room tmpriture logs to make candies
