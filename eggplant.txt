Eggplant Parmesan Rolls

6 servings

2 eggplants (about 2 1/4 lbs.) cut lengthwise into 1/4" slices
1 lb. swiss chard, Center ribs removed
2 eggs
15 oz. ricotta cheese
1 1/4 cups grated Parmesan cheese, divided
2 Tbsp. chopped fresh mint
3/4 tsp. ground black pepper
15 to 16  oz. canned tomato sauce
8 oz thinly sliced water packed Mozzarella

salt eggplant and let drain for 30-60 minutes, rinse and pat dry.

brush eggplant with olive oil and broil 5-6 " from heat till brow,  3-4 minutes.

boil chard 2 minutes in salted water, drain & rinse in cold water. squeeze out water and dry

Whisk eggs w/ pinch of salt stir in chard & ricotta, 1 cup Parmesan, mint & black pepper.

Oil 15 x 10 x 2 glass baking dish. Spread half tomato sauce over bottom.
 put 1 heaping Tbsp. chard mixture on each eggplant slice and roll up.

Arrange rolls seam down in baking dish.
cover with remaining tomato sauce.
cover with single layer of mozzarella. sprinkle w/ 1/4 cup parmesan.

Can be held 1 day coverd w/ foil and chilled.

heat oven to 350.

Bake covered with foil 30 minutes if fresh, 40 if chilled.

uncover and bake until bubbling and brown spots  15 - 20 minutes.
