Baked ricotta with lemmon and chives

From Pucker: schoolbook for citrus lovers, by Gwendolyn Richards

Untested

2 cus ricotta
2 eggs, beaten
Zest of 1 lemon
1 clove garlic, minced
1/2 cup grated Parmesan
2 Tbsp chopped chives
1/8 tsp chili flakes
3 Tbsp olive oil

Preheat oven to 375

Butter baking dish or ramekins to hold 3 cups

Mix ricotta, eggs,zest, garlic, Parmesan, chives, chili flakes, & 2 Tbsp olive oil. Stir.
Spoon into prepairedness diss(s), smooth top.

Bake u till slightly brown on top. Time may very, check in 15 minutes.

Top w/ remaining g olive oil.

Serve with bread or crackers.
