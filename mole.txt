MOLE SAUCE

1 large onion, chopped
2 cloves Garlic, chopped
3 Tbsp. Oil
4 Tbsp. Sugar
3 Tbsp Flour
1 tsp. Salt
2 tsp. Cinnamon
4 tsp. Chili powder
1 tsp. Ground cumin
2 tsp oregano
1 28 oz can tomatoes
1 cup chicken broth
3 oz. Unsweetened baking chocolate

Blend flour, sugar, & spices.

Saute onion & garlic in oil until translucent.

Add spice mixture to onion mixture and cook one minute

Add Broth and tomatoes

Bring to boil

Add chocolate stir until chocolate melts.

Simmer 5 minutes stirring frequently.
