Tori No Mizutaki (Japanese Chicken Soup)
-----------------------------------------

Cook 1 chicken in 4 cups of water and remove bones

Add 
3 Tbsp. Lemon Juice
4 Tbsp Shoyu
1 Tbsp. salt

Add vegetables (carrots, Celery, Onion, leeks, etc.)
add scallion and mushrooms just before serving.
