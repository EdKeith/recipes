Mexican Red Rice

from Simply Mexican by Lourdes Castro

Untested

2 large tomatoes, cored & quartered
1 onion, quartered
2 cloves garlic
1 jalapeno, stemmed
3 Tbsp olive oil
2 cups long grain white rice
1 1/2 cups chicken broth or water
2 tsp. salt

puree tomatoes, onion, garlic, & jalapeno in blender.

heat oil in sauce pan over medium high heat
add rice & stir 2 minutes, until rice is completely coated in oil and begins
to releae a toasty aroma.

add pureed vegetables, stir until well blended.

add chicken broth & salt and bring to simmer covered 30 minutes.

Remove from heat and let sit covered 5 minutes then fluff with fork.

serves 4
