Cheese Stuffed Baked Potatoes
-----------------------------

4 Large warm baked potatoes
3/4 cup diced cheddar cheese
1/2 cup sour cream
1/4 cup butter
1/2 cup cooked bacon, crumbled
2 - 4 TBSP half-and Half
1 tsp seasoned salt
1/8 tsp pepper
1/4 cup grated cheddar cheese

cut potatoes in half lengthwise and scoop out

beat potatoes, sour cream, butter, 2TBS Half & Half, salt, & pepper
until smooth, adding additional half & half if needed.
Mixture should be stiff and hold shape.

Cool, then fold in cheese cubes and bacon.

Put in potato shells and sprinkle grated cheese on top.

May be refrigerated up to 24 hour or frozen.

Bake at 400 deg. F 30 min. if chilled,  45 min. if frozen,
or microwave before serving
