Goo - Homemade sports gell

from Ultralight Backpacking' Tips by Mike Clelland

untested

2 Cups brown rice syrup
1/4 cup cashew butter
1/4 protein powder (vinella seems best)
pinch of salt
dash of hot water

warm brown rice syrup in tub of hat water for 1/2 an hour to thin so it will not burn out the blender

poursyrup into blender (add a little hot water if it is too thick)

slowly add cashew butter and protein powder and salt.

store in 500ml Platypus or marathon runner's gel flask (fill with funnel while still warm)
