Cheese Ball
-----------

8 oz. Cream cheese
1/4 lb. cheddar cheese shredded
1/4 to 1/2 lb. Blue cheese, crumbled
1/3 cup chopped pecans
1/4 cup parsley, chopped
1.5 tsp Worcestershire sauce
1.5 tsp onion minced.. 

Blend everything but nuts & parsley form into ball & chill

Remove from refrigerator 20-30 min before serving.

roll in nuts & parsley.
